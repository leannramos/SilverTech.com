Project Info

Credit Union of Texas (CUTX), headquartered in Dallas, Texas, and serving all 50 states, engaged with SilverTech to create a full, end-to-end redesign and rebuild of their website. This redesign included a personalized and predictive experience across the entire customer journey.

Main Objectives

  • Grow the Credit Union through converting prospects into new members
  • Driving additional products with existing members
  • Leverage the CMS and data to provide more relevant communications
  • Leverage data and analytics to drive growth and revenue
BACK TO OUR WORK

The Process

SilverTech worked with CUTX to identify that the best strategy to reach their goals was to determine a path that would create a seamless experience for the user across all touch points on the customer journey. They also, needed to integrate live information, knowledge base articles, data, and live loan rates. Using first, second, and third-party data, CUTX prospects were immediately segmented into personas based on the products and services offered. The segmented personas and personalized Calls to Action drove site visitors down their intended web journey.
